Lesson Components¶

Most lessons include:

  • Overview
  • Required Viewing
  • Required Reading
  • Code Talk (discussion about the week’s work)
  • Assignment(s)

Some lessons will also include ungraded activities.

Grading Policy

This course is graded as Satisfactory Completion (SC) or Unsatisfactory Completion (USC). You must complete the course with a minimum of 80% total to earn a SC.

Your grade is comprised of the following:

Category Percent
Attendance and Participation 10%
Activities 25%
Quizzes 25%
Assignments 40%
