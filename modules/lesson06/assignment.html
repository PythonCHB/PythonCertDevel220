Lesson 6 : Assignment¶

In this lesson’s assignment we are going to apply techniques to identify the right way to find bottlenecks in modules. We will use an evidence based approach to making the right improvements

Here is what you need to do:¶

  1. Take an instructor provided module and identify the bottlenecks.
  2. Rewrite the module to improve performance. Provide evidence to demonstrate the improvement.
  3. Asses and compare the performance of adding 100 records to the product and customer databases, and reading 100 records from these databases.
